About Me

Hi, I'm Sariah. I was an art major
before I became a CS major.
After 5 years of false career starts,
I began taking online classes in
web development.

My introduction to Javascript made me
want to learn programming full-time.
The idea that I could mix innovative
technology with a creative mindset
inspired me to pursue software engineering
as a career.

When I'm not learning programming, I am a
wife, a dog mom, and a world traveller. I
love all creative outlets like woodworking,
painting, sewing, and pottery. I enjoy
eating and exercising. I also love hiking,
learning new words, and doing puzzles.

I grew up in Ohio as the youngest of
five children. I went to University in Utah.
Now I live near Seattle with my huband and
our dog, Kiba, and I am back in school at
Oregon State University.

Feel free to:

peruse my work,

view my travel blog,

or contact me.
